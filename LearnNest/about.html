Our Journey

From a small classroom idea to a comprehensive learning platform

2018

The Beginning

Founded by a group of educators and technologists who saw the need for a more integrated digital learning platform. Started as a small project to help students track grades and assignments.

2019

First Major Release

Launched our first complete platform with grade tracking, digital library, and basic club features. Partnered with 10 schools and reached 1,000 students in the first year.

2020

Remote Learning Expansion

Expanded rapidly during the pandemic to support remote learning. Added video library, interactive assignments, and virtual clubs. Reached 10,000 students across 50 schools.

2022

Platform Enhancement

Redesigned the entire platform with modern UI/UX, added AI-powered learning recommendations, and launched mobile apps. Expanded to 200+ schools and 50,000+ students.

2023 - Present

Future Vision

Continuing to innovate with virtual reality classrooms, advanced analytics for personalized learning paths, and expanding our global reach to make quality education accessible to all.
